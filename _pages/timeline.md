## Timeline 

### Competition Phase
- **July**  
  - 🚀 **Competition Launch**: Competition and website go live.  
  - 🛠 **Development Phase Opens**: Participants access training and initial test datasets to begin developing their models.

- **Early September**  
  - 🛠 **Development Phase Closes** 
  - 🔓 **Final Evaluation Phase Opens**: Held-out test dataset is released for final submissions.

- **End of September**  
  - 🏁 **Competition Closes**: Final submissions are collected for evaluation.

### Post-Competition Phase
- **October**  
  - 📊 **Evaluation**: Submissions are reviewed and results are finalized.

- **November**  
  - 🏆 **Winners Announced**: Top participants are notified.  
  - ✉️ **Invitations Sent**: Winners are invited to present at the NeurIPS competition workshop.

- **December**  
  - 🎤 **NeurIPS Workshop**: Winners and top participants present their work at the NeurIPS competition workshop.
